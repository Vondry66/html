The production of the third generation started in 1992 and (very sadly) ended in 2002.
Rumors are however, that Mazda want's to revive the rotary and maybe bring back something similar. Which would be great because that would be another cute baby I can't afford.
The final model (2002) was powered by 206 KW engine.

It was one of the first twin-turbocharged engines in commercial use.
The first turbo was low-rev. It made sure that the car goes vvvvvrap-rap-prap-prap-prap straight from idle.
The second turbo was high-rev. Which as you can guess by now, made this car a high-rpm beast.
